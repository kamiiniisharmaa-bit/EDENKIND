Privacy Pledge

Effective: June 1, 2026  |  Revised for clarity & care

At EDENKIND, we honor your trust like a rare artifact. This Privacy Pledge describes how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you share with us when visiting our digital cabinet, purchasing objects, or subscribing to our epistles. Transparency is our cornerstone — no hidden chambers, no obscure data dealings.

We do not and will never sell your personal data to third parties. Your story remains yours.

1. What information we collect

We collect only what is necessary to honor your transaction and nurture our community. Categories include:

TypeExamplesPurpose
Identity & ContactName, email, postal address, phone numberOrder fulfilment, shipping, customer care
Transaction dataPurchase history, wishlist, returnsProcess payments, manage loyalty, improve curation
Technical dataIP address, browser type, device identifiersSite security, analytics, performance
CommunicationsEmail correspondence, support messagesRespond to inquiries, provide updates

If you interact with our studio via social media or events, we may collect information you choose to share (e.g., contest entries). We never request sensitive data (e.g., health information, political beliefs).

2. How we use your information

EDENKIND uses personal data solely for legitimate purposes tied to our mission of slow commerce and community building:

  • Processing and delivering your orders, including payment and shipping notifications.
  • Sending you studio updates, restock alerts, and artisan stories (only with your consent).
  • Improving our website, personalizing recommendations, and preventing fraud.
  • Responding to customer service requests and supporting returns/exchanges.
Our epistles (newsletters): You may unsubscribe anytime via the link in every email. We promise low-frequency, high-wonder letters.

3. Sharing & disclosure

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Limited data sharing occurs only with:

  • Service partners: Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal), shipping carriers (USPS, FedEx), and email platforms (Klaviyo) — all contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights and safety of EDENKIND, our users, or the public.
  • Business transitions: In the event of a merger or acquisition, you will be notified before any transfer of data.

We may share aggregated, anonymized statistics for internal insights, but such data cannot identify you.

4. Cookies & similar technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember cart items, and analyze site traffic. You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Types we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for checkout, login, and security.
  • Functional cookies: Remember language preferences and region.
  • Analytics cookies: Via privacy-respecting tools (like Plausible or anonymized Google Analytics).

Blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality. Third-party embeds (e.g., videos) may set their own cookies — we limit these where possible.

5. Your rights & choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations).
  • Restriction/Opt-out: Limit processing or withdraw consent for marketing.
  • Portability: Receive a structured, machine-readable copy of your data.

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@edenkind.com. We will respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

California & GDPR notice: If you are a California resident, see our CCPA addendum below. For EU/UK residents, lawful bases include contract performance, legitimate interest (fraud prevention), and consent where required.

6. Data security & retention

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — SSL encryption, restricted access, and regular vulnerability scans — to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we strive to use industry best practices.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, including legal obligations (e.g., tax records for 7 years). After that, we securely anonymize or delete your information.

7. Children's privacy

EDENKIND does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete that information.

8. International data transfers

Our studio is based in the United States. If you access EDENKIND from outside the US, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the US where our servers are located. We rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or adequacy decisions where applicable to ensure an equivalent level of protection.

9. Changes to this Privacy Pledge

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The “Effective” date at the top indicates when the latest version was published. Material changes will be notified via email (if you have an account) or a banner on our site. Your continued use of EDENKIND after modifications signifies acceptance.

This Privacy Pledge is part of EDENKIND’s commitment to radical transparency. For any privacy-specific questions, contact our Data Curator at privacy@edenkind.com or via post: EDENKIND Privacy, 1470 Walnut Grove, San Francisco, CA 94102.
